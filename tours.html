Questions

Frequently Asked

What's included in the tour price?+
The 7-day tour includes your 4-star hotel for 6 nights, two social events with food and drinks, two group day-tours, transport to all socials and tours, translators at the socials, airport pickup on arrival and a day-one welcome briefing. See the full "What's Included" section above.
Do I need to speak Spanish?+
No. Translators are included at every social event and we provide translation technology as a baseline. A dedicated personal translator is available as an add-on for one-on-one dates — especially handy if you're a little shy.
How many women will I meet?+
Each social event brings 100+ verified single women, and you attend two of them on the 7-day tour — plus the one-on-one dates our team helps arrange throughout the week.
Are the women real and verified?+
Yes. Every woman is met and verified in person by our team in Colombia. Real profiles, real people, real intentions — no fake accounts.
What if I don't match with anyone?+
Our contact guarantee means you will meet women in person. The socials are designed to be a genuinely fun night out regardless — most guests leave having made several connections.
Can you help with flights, visas or staying longer?+
Yes — flights, visa support, private tours, extended stays and more are available on request through our optional services and VIP concierge. Just ask our team.
