Best practices

How to promote MatchLatina

Keep it authentic and honest — that's what converts best and protects the brand.

Do this

Show the destinations — Colombia's cities, culture and lifestyle.
Talk about it as a premium travel & social experience.
Use the official photos and videos we provide.
Add your link to your bio, stories and video descriptions.

Avoid this

Don't make promises we can't keep (guaranteed marriage, etc.).
Don't present it in a vulgar or misleading way.
Don't spam links where they're not allowed.
Don't use paid ads on our brand name without approval.
