FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does MatchLatina work?
+
You browse our directory of verified Colombian women, submit an inquiry form, and our team contacts you personally to discuss your options. We then organize your trip to Colombia where you'll attend exclusive social events and meet women in person.
Are the women's profiles real and verified?
+
Yes. Every woman on MatchLatina is personally verified by our team on the ground in Colombia. We conduct in-person or video interviews and confirm their identity before adding them to our directory.
What is included in the tour price?
+
Hotel accommodation, airport transfers, organized social events, translators, and personal introductions. Flights and personal expenses are not included. Full details are listed on each tour page.
Do I need to speak Spanish?
+
No. Our translators are present at all events and introductions. Many of our women also speak basic to fluent English.
How many women will I meet?
+
Our tours are small and exclusive — typically 8–15 men per group with a minimum 7:1 women-to-men ratio at our social events. You will have multiple personal introductions during your stay.
How much does it cost?
+
Tour prices vary by destination and duration. Please visit our Tours page or submit an inquiry and our team will send you full pricing details.
Is this different from online dating sites?
+
Yes — completely. Instead of endless online chatting, we focus on real, in-person meetings: a carefully organized trip to Colombia where you meet verified women face to face at exclusive events.
